About KeshavSoft

Empowering the next generation of web developers

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing hands-on, practical training in modern web development technologies.

KeshavSoft was founded with a vision to empower B.Tech students with real-world skills that make them job-ready. We focus on practical learning through structured internships, live projects, and industry-standard deployment practices.
